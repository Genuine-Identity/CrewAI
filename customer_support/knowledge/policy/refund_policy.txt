REFUND POLICY

Refunds are issued once returned items are inspected and approved.

Processing Time:
- 5–7 business days after item inspection
- Bank processing may take additional 3–5 days

Partial Refunds May Apply If:
- Item is returned damaged due to misuse
- Item is missing components
- Return is outside the 30-day window

Fraud Prevention:
Customers with high refund rates may be subject to investigation.
Refund abuse may result in account suspension.

Refund Methods:
- Credit/Debit Card: Original payment method
- Store Credit: Immediately available
- PayPal: Original PayPal account
