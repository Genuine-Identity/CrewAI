SHIPPING POLICY

Processing Time:
Orders are processed within 2–3 business days.

Shipping Options:
- Standard (5–7 business days)
- Express (2–3 business days)
- Overnight (1 business day)

Shipping Costs:
Calculated at checkout based on weight and location.

International Shipping:
Customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the customer.

Lost Packages:
Customers must report lost packages within 7 days of expected delivery date.
