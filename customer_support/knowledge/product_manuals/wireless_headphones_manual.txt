PRODUCT MANUAL
Model: SoundWave Pro Wireless Headphones

OVERVIEW
Noise-cancelling Bluetooth headphones with 30-hour battery life.

SPECIFICATIONS
- Bluetooth 5.3
- Battery: 30 hours
- Charging: USB-C
- Noise Cancellation: Active Hybrid ANC

PAIRING INSTRUCTIONS
1. Hold power button for 5 seconds.
2. Enable Bluetooth on device.
3. Select “SoundWave Pro”.

RESET INSTRUCTIONS
Hold volume up + power button for 10 seconds.

TROUBLESHOOTING

No Sound:
- Ensure connected to correct device.
- Increase volume.
- Restart headphones.

Connection Drops:
- Stay within 10 meters.
- Remove interference.
- Reset device.

Charging Issues:
- Use original cable.
- Try different USB port.

WARRANTY
1-Year Limited Warranty.
Does not cover water damage or physical impact.

SUPPORT
Contact support if:
- One earcup stops working
- Charging port becomes loose
