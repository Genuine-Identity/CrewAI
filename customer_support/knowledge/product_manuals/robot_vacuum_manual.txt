PRODUCT MANUAL
Model: CleanBot X200

OVERVIEW
Smart robotic vacuum with mapping technology.

SPECIFICATIONS
- Battery Life: 120 minutes
- Dustbin: 600ml
- App Control: Yes
- WiFi Required: 2.4GHz

INITIAL SETUP
1. Charge for 4 hours.
2. Download CleanBot app.
3. Connect to WiFi.
4. Start mapping mode.

MAINTENANCE
- Empty dustbin after each use.
- Clean filter weekly.
- Replace brush every 3 months.

TROUBLESHOOTING

Vacuum Not Charging:
- Clean charging contacts.
- Ensure dock is plugged in.

Not Connecting to WiFi:
- Use 2.4GHz network.
- Reset device.
- Restart router.

Stops Mid-Clean:
- Check for obstacles.
- Clean wheels.
- Ensure battery is charged.

WARRANTY
1-Year Limited Warranty.
Excludes battery wear and tear.

SUPPORT
Contact support if:
- Mapping fails repeatedly
- Device emits unusual noise
- Arrived damaged
