1. Connect the power adapter.

CHARGING
- Use only the original charger.
- Full charge takes approximately 2 hours.
- Avoid draining battery to 0% regularly.

TROUBLESHOOTING

Device Not Powering On:
- Ensure battery is charged.
- Hold power button for 10 seconds to force reset.
- Try different power outlet.

Overheating:
- Ensure ventilation ports are not blocked.
- Avoid placing laptop on soft surfaces.
- Update system drivers.

Screen Flickering:
- Update graphics drivers.
- Restart device.
- Check display cable (if external monitor).

SAFETY INFORMATION
- Do not expose to water.
- Avoid extreme temperatures.
- Do not open internal components.
