PRODUCT MANUAL
Model: Smartphone X

OVERVIEW
Smartphone X is a 5G-enabled device with OLED display and AI-enhanced camera.

SPECIFICATIONS
- Display: 6.5” OLED
- Storage: 256GB
- Camera: 50MP + 12MP Dual Lens
- Battery: 4500mAh
- Charging: Fast Charge 65W

INITIAL SETUP
1. Insert SIM card.
2. Power on device.
3. Connect to WiFi.
4. Sign in with Google or Apple ID.

BATTERY CARE
- Avoid overcharging overnight.
- Use certified charging cables only.
- Optimal battery range: 20%–80%.

TROUBLESHOOTING

Phone Not Charging:
- Clean charging port.
- Try different cable.
- Restart phone.

App Crashes:
- Update app.
- Clear app cache.
- Reinstall application.

Device Overheating:
- Avoid direct sunlight.
- Close background apps.
- Update system software.

FACTORY RESET
Settings → System → Reset → Factory Data Reset

WARRANTY
1-Year Manufacturer Warranty.
Battery covered for 6 months.
Physical or water damage not covered.

SUPPORT CONTACT
If:
- Screen flickers persistently
- Phone shuts down randomly
- Device arrived damaged
